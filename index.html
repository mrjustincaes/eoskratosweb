Infrastructure & Services

Comprehensive energy asset management, structural hardware deployment, and dynamic technical utility operations.

Solar Hybrid Mini-Grids

Deploying sub-1MW localized solar infrastructure that operates independently of the national grid, utilizing high-cycle lithium iron phosphate (LFP) battery storage to guarantee 24/7 uptime.

Smart Pre-Paid Metering

Executing energy sale agreements utilizing proprietary token-based smart meters. This automated utility distribution model ensures complete operational efficiency and eliminates accounts receivable risks.

Distributed Energy Resources (DER)

Designing decentralized power systems, combined heating and cooling solutions, and behind-the-meter storage to enhance local energy resilience and minimize dependency on centralized infrastructure.

Energy Asset Management (O&M)

Providing end-to-end lifecycle management of solar plants. This includes predictive maintenance, continuous monitoring via centralized operations, regulatory compliance, and performance ratio optimization to ensure maximum ROI.

Technical Engineering Procurement

Delivering heavy electrical engineering, civil works, and installation services. We source Tier-1 monocrystalline photovoltaic modules and grid-forming inverters optimized for continuous industrial load profiles.

Commercial Sustainability Optimization

Assisting large-scale operations in reaching carbon-reduction goals while managing capital costs through precise technical integrations and localized emission displacement.
