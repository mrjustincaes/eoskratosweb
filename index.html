Our Infrastructure

We provide solar hybrid mini-grids, smart metering, and EPC engineering services.

Solar Hybrid Mini-Grids

Deploying sub-1MW localized solar infrastructure that operates independently of the national grid, utilizing high-cycle lithium iron phosphate (LFP) battery storage to guarantee 24/7 uptime.

Smart Pre-Paid Metering

Executing energy sale agreements utilizing proprietary token-based smart meters. This automated utility distribution model ensures complete operational efficiency and eliminates accounts receivable risks.

Energy Asset Management

Providing end-to-end lifecycle management of solar plants. This includes predictive maintenance, continuous monitoring via centralized operations, regulatory compliance, and performance ratio optimization.
