Eos Kratos LTD

Bringing the Dawn of Reliable Energy

Eos Kratos LTD is an independent power producer incorporated in Nigeria, engineered to address the chronic electricity deficit in the Idu Karmo district of Abuja, Federal Capital Territory.

Strategic Pillars

The national grid consistently delivers less than 5,000 MW of functional power. In Idu Karmo, non-priority zones experience outage hours frequently exceeding 16–20 hours. Commercial and residential customers spend significantly on diesel-powered generators at exorbitant effective costs.

We deploy localized solar hybrid mini-grids that operate entirely independently of the Nigerian national grid, delivering reliable, commercially priced electricity directly to tenants and high-density residential end-users. Our business model is anchored on four pillars:

Regulatory Efficiency

Sub-1MW infrastructure design qualifies for the NERC Mini-Grid Permit regime, bypassing full generation license requirements, mandatory environmental impact assessments, and accelerating NEMSA safety certifications.

Revenue Certainty

Prepaid smart metering eliminates accounts receivable risk. Customers purchase credit tokens in advance, and meters automatically disconnect upon credit exhaustion.

Market Exclusivity

Secured through Tripartite Agreements with local community leadership, legally restricting incumbent distribution companies from extending service into the contracted community zone.

Capital Flexibility

Routing initial procurement through existing US-registered corporate vehicles allows for seamless international hardware acquisition and limits local currency depreciation risks during CAPEX phases.
